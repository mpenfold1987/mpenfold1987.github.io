Chapter I

The Beginning

It is a time of great unrest. Freedom fighters, emerging from hidden locations, have achieved their first triumph against a powerful oppressive regime.

During the confrontation, courageous agents succeeded in capturing vital information about the regime's ultimate weapon, a colossal space fortress with unmatched destructive capability.

Pursued by the regime's sinister agents, the freedom fighters race to deliver the plans that can bring an end to the tyranny and restore peace to the galaxy...
